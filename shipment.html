Shipping Information


Free Shipping
We are pleased to offer FREE SHIPPING on all eligible orders. Most items ship to most destinations for free! Once you provide your shipping address during the checkout process, Zoe's will display any shipping charges on the final checkout page. Shipping charges are based on a per-item basis.
Most orders are processed within 24 - 48 hours and will arrive within 1-4 business days!

In-store Pick-up
It is our pleasure to offer our valued customers the option of shipping your order for your convenient pickup to any Zoe's location across Canada for FREE. However, if an item is below $9.99 or a Clearance Item, we are able to offer discounted shipping of only $3.99 to a Zoe's of your choice! Simply place an order on Zoes.com as normal and select "In-Store Pickup"!
If the item being purchased on Zoe.com is currently already in stock at your selected Zoe's pickup store, the $3.99 in-store pickup shipping fee is waived, and the in-store pickup option is FREE. The only case where a $3.99 charge applies is when the item is not available or sold in the location you are selecting.
*Most orders are available for pickup within 48-72 hours once shipped to the store. Some items may take longer if they are shipping to or from remote Zoe's locations.
*Due to courier hours of operations, all weekends and holidays are not included in estimated shipping times.

Free Returns
Any online order placed on Zoe's.com which qualifies for FREE SHIPPING will also qualify for FREE RETURN SHIPPING. Qualifying orders will receive a prepaid return postage label.
To initiate a return, simply visit our Order Tracking page and follow the steps on the "Return Order" tab. Alternatively, you may contact our Zoe's Customer Care team and we will gladly assist you.

24 Hour Express
Please note that deliveries are not available on Saturday nor Sunday.
